🍽️ FridgeAI

Your smart kitchen assistant — from fridge to plate.

📖 Description
FridgeAI is an AI-powered web application that transforms the way you cook. Simply take a photo of your fridge, and FridgeAI will automatically detect the ingredients you have, suggest personalized recipes, identify missing ingredients, and even help you order them directly from your favorite supermarket.
Built with a focus on reducing food waste, saving time, and making cooking accessible to everyone — FridgeAI bridges the gap between your fridge and your next delicious meal.

✨ Features

📸 Fridge Scanning — Upload a photo and let AI detect your ingredients
🍳 Recipe Suggestions — Get personalized recipes based on what you have
🛒 Smart Shopping — Identify and order missing ingredients (Marjane / Carrefour)
💰 Budget & Planning — Plan your meals and manage your grocery budget
🤖 AI-Powered — Powered by Computer Vision & Claude AI


🛠️ Tech Stack
Python FastAPI React.js TensorFlow Claude API PostgreSQL
